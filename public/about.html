About

At Luna Library, we believe that all literature deserves a space where readers can easily find and sort their favorites. Our catalogue is designed to help you search, sort, and explore books effortlessly. Whether you're a casual reader or an avid book collector our platform is built to make managing your reading list simple and enjoyable.


Features include:
  • Book Info: See details like title, author, cover, genre, and more.
  • Sorting & Filtering: Sort books by rating, pages, words, and reading status.
  • PWA Features: Offline access, fast loading, and a seamless experience across devices.
Note: Books in the catalogue are pre-added by the system and cannot be uploaded by users. Join us in building a community of book lovers and make your reading experience better than ever!
