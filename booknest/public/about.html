About BookNest

Welcome to BookNest, your friendly school book club companion designed to bring students together around a shared love of reading! BookNest makes it easy to engage with and vote on new books while earning points and friendly competition between guilds.

What is BookNest?

BookNest is a web app that centralises book selection and encourages reading through interactive features. Students can explore book polls, vote for their favourite next read, participate in fun reading quests, and track their pointsâ€”all within one platform.

Key Features

  • Voting Polls: Students vote on upcoming books to help decide the next book the club will read together.
  • Quests & Challenges: Fun reading challenges that reward points and motivate consistent engagement.
  • Guild Competition: Earn points for your guild by participating in quests, fostering teamwork and friendly rivalry.
  • Leaderboards: See how your guild and fellow readers stack up with ongoing scoreboards.

Why BookNest?

BookNest was created to encourage reading in a fun, collaborative way. By combining voting, challenges, and guilds, it turns reading from a solo activity into an engaging social experience. Whether you're a casual reader or a bookworm, BookNest has something to keep you inspired and connected.

Getting Started

Simply register, join a guild, and start exploring the current book poll and quests. Cast your vote, complete quests, and watch your points grow as you climb the leaderboard. Happy reading!
